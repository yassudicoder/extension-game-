# Credits & asset licenses

All raw art packs live under `src/assets/scene/` and are **git-ignored** — they are
**not** redistributed in this repository. The built/deployed site (`dist/`) bakes the
art into the page. Before any public or commercial deployment, confirm each pack's
license below allows it.

> ⚠️ **Licensing needs review.** The current build uses art from packs with mixed and,
> in places, unconfirmed terms. Notably the Sprout Lands basic pack is **non-commercial**
> and the PixelFarmDEMO animals have **no license file** in the download. See per-pack
> notes before launching.

## In active use

### Farm scenery — Sprout Lands (Cup Nooble)
Buildings, terrain, fences, plants and props.
<https://cupnooble.itch.io/sprout-lands-asset-pack>
Per the `read_me.txt` shipped with the basic pack:
- **Non-commercial use only.** (NFT and AI-training use are explicitly disallowed.)
- Modification allowed; **credit to Cup Nooble required**.
- The pack itself may **not** be redistributed/resold (we git-ignore it — OK).
- For open-source projects, include a note that the assets are by Cup Nooble with the
  license terms (this file).

### Animated animals — Pixel Farm Demo (by Pixels by Skab)
The grazing **cow** and the pecking/roaming **chickens**.
**Pixels by Skab** — <https://pixelsbyskab.itch.io/pixel-farm-demo>
- "Name your own price" pack; the creator states **no generative AI was used** to make it.
- ⚠️ The page and download include **no explicit written license** (commercial use,
  attribution, redistribution all unstated). Credit is given here as a courtesy; **confirm
  usage terms with the creator before any commercial deployment.**
