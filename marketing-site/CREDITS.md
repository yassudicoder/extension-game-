# Credits

## Farm pixel art — Sprout Lands
The farm scenery (buildings, terrain, fences, plants, props) uses the **Sprout Lands**
tileset by **Cup Nooble** — <https://cupnooble.itch.io/sprout-lands-asset-pack>.
Used under its "free for commercial use" terms. The raw pack files are **not**
redistributed in this repository — they're git-ignored; download the pack into
`src/assets/scene/` to build locally (see that folder's README). The built/deployed
site bakes the art into `dist/`.
