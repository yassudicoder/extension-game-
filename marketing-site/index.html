your companions

Three friends, each with their own ways

One little friend wanders your open tabs — idling, ambling, hopping over for a pet. Choose whose company you want today; the rest wait cozily back at the farm.

Only one pet is “out” on your pages at a time, so your tabs stay calm — never crowded.

a home of your own

A farm that grows with you

Every glass of water and every good night’s sleep adds up. Spend your Biscuits at the market and the homestead fills in — more friends in the coop, comfier beds, a fuller little farm to come home to.

The better you look after yourself, the more alive the place becomes.
